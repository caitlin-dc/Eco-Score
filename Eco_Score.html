    THE SOLUTION IS TECH

    Environmental Solutions Shared Together

    1. Find Product Eco-Score

    The most basic feature of the application is the ability to track and understand your product's Eco-Score. The Eco-Score is based on manufacturing practices, materials and ingredients, and packaging.

    2. Input Your Products

    Going a step further, the software implements the ability for users to add their own items and input the metrics of these items. This also adds the item to our database for a constantly growing collection.

    3. Constantly Improving

    Through user-input, the software will use machine learning algorithms and data analysis to understand products and sustainability to give user's valuable insights and recommendations.
